How It Works
Formula: CFD Zone = Futures Zone + Basis
Basis = Current CFD Price − Current Futures Price
Stability: FX basis moves 1–3 pips/day outside rolls. Check weekly.
Roll Warning: Basis jumps 10–15+ pips = contract roll. Re-read before translating zones.
Indices/Metals: Basis is larger (points) but equally stable within a contract.

Institutional Filters — How to Use Them for Decisions
WHAT EACH FILTER TELLS YOU
Filter What You Enter What It Tells You
Spec COT %
Col B		 0–100 percentile of speculative net position Where specs are positioned relative to history. Below 15 = max short (contrarian bullish). Above 85 = max long (contrarian bearish).
Commercial COT %
Col F		 0–100 percentile of commercial hedger net position Where the smart money is. Above 75 = hedgers accumulating aggressively (prices cheap to them). Below 25 = distributing (prices expensive). This confirms or questions the spec signal.
Z-Score
Col G		 Standard deviations from mean (auto-calculated from Col B) Normalises positioning for regime changes. -2σ is statistically extreme short regardless of era. More precise than raw percentile when position sizes have shifted over time.
Open Interest OI direction + Price direction (week-on-week) Reveals WHO is driving the move. Rising price + Rising OI = new institutional longs entering (strong). Rising price + Falling OI = short covering (weak, may reverse).
Yield Spread Momentum Current spread % + 4-week-ago spread % Rate of change of the carry differential. Fast-expanding carry (+8 to +10) = strong institutional tailwind. Contracting carry = headwind even if level is still positive.
HOW TO COMBINE THEM — DECISION FRAMEWORK
🟢 HIGH CONVICTION LONG (Score 70–100)
Spec COT <15 + Commercial >75 + Z-Score <-2 + OI Accumulation + Yield Expanding
→ Full position size. All five signals aligned. Rare — when it happens, this is your highest-R trade.
🔵 BULLISH (Score 55–70)
3–4 filters bullish, 1–2 neutral or missing
→ Standard position size. Good setup but not maximum conviction. Use your Extreme or Momentum entry type.
⚪ NEUTRAL (Score 45–55)
Filters mixed or pointing in different directions
→ Wait. The Conflict phase. No edge — preserve capital until filters align.
🔴 HIGH CONVICTION SHORT (Score 0–30)
Spec COT >85 + Commercial <25 + Z-Score >+2 + OI Distribution + Yield Contracting
→ Full position size short. Crowded carry trade unwinding — highest-R short setup.

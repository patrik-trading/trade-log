Daily Trigger — Entry Sequence
1
Weekly bias confirmed Saturday — COT, yield, inst score all checked
The decision is made on Saturday. Monday is just execution.
2
Daily raw percentile below 10% (long) or above 90% (short)
Entry timing window open. Not a trigger — just permission to look for entry.
3
Identify 4H demand (long) or supply (short) zone on unadjusted futures chart
Where did price spend least time before moving away aggressively? Mark top and bottom.
4
Translate zone to CFD using saved basis in Zone Translator tab
Use Weekday mode. Enter futures zone levels → CFD levels calculated instantly.
5
Place limit order inside CFD zone — let price come to you
Stop below zone bottom (long) or above zone top (short). Zone defines the stop.
6
Daily 21,3 hook confirms (optional but preferred)
Hook = raw crosses above smoothed from below 10. If it fired Friday, enter Monday open.
Daily Entry Types
Weekly bias determines direction and size. The daily chart determines when to enter. Two valid entry types — usable on any weekly setup regardless of phase.
Type 1 — Extreme Reversal
Indicator: 21,3 Tactical
Trigger: Raw below 10% (long) or above 90% (short) + hook forming
Entry: At 4H demand / supply zone
Character: Earlier entry — catches more of the move, higher risk of being early
Long — raw crosses above smoothed from below 10
Short — raw crosses below smoothed from above 90
Type 2 — Momentum Confirmation
Indicator: 65,5 Strategic
Trigger: Crosses above 65 (long) or below 35 (short)
Entry: On the cross or first pullback after
Character: Later entry — direction confirmed, less risk of being early but smaller potential move
Long — 65,5 line crosses above 65
Short — 65,5 line crosses below 35
How to choose: Price at zone + 21,3 extreme → Type 1. Price bounced but 65,5 not yet crossed → wait for Type 2. Never force both on the same pair at the same time.
Execution Rules
DO
Place limit orders at the zone — wait
Use previous week high/low as reference
Enter at London open for best fills
Check order is still valid after weekend gap
Log the trade immediately after fill
Use unadjusted futures for zone identification
Use adjusted futures for indicator reading
DON'T
Chase entry after hook fires — wait for retest
Move stop closer during the trade
Add filters at execution time
Trade Conflict phase regardless of inst score
Size up beyond 1R on any single trade
Overtrade — one clean setup beats three mediocre
Use EMA on price for entry — that's retail bias
Reversal Confirmation Patterns — Visual Reference
Look for these patterns forming at your 4H demand or supply zone after the daily raw percentile enters the trigger window.
1
Bullish Engulfing
↑ BULLISH — STRONGEST SINGLE SIGNAL
Body engulfs previous red candle entirely. Opens below prior close, closes above prior open. Institutional buyers overwhelmed sellers in one session. Enter on close.
★★★★★
2
Hammer
↑ BULLISH — ZONE REJECTION
Small body at top, wick 2-3x the body. Market tested lower, rejected completely. Most powerful when forming directly at a demand zone after a sustained decline.
★★★★☆
3
Morning Star
↑ BULLISH — 3-CANDLE CONFIRMATION
Large red → small doji → large green. Third candle must close well into the first. Takes 3 sessions. Rarest but most reliable — worth waiting for at key zones.
★★★★★
4
Shooting Star
↓ BEARISH — ZONE REJECTION
Mirror of hammer. Small body at bottom, long upper wick 2-3x body. Market tested higher at supply zone, got completely rejected. Bearish continuation expected.
★★★★☆
5
Bearish Engulfing
↓ BEARISH — INSTITUTIONAL SELLING
Red body engulfs previous green candle entirely. Opens above prior close, closes below prior open. Sellers overwhelmed buyers at supply zone in one session. Enter on close.
★★★★★
6
Doji at Zone
◈ INDECISION — EARLY WARNING ONLY
Equal open and close — pure indecision. Meaningful only at key zones after a sustained move. Always wait for the next candle to confirm direction before entering.
★★★☆☆
Key principle: Every pattern is only meaningful at a structurally significant level — a 4H demand or supply zone on the unadjusted futures chart.  |  Best combo: Inst. score 65+ · COT extreme · Pattern at 4H zone · Daily hook aligning  |  Engulfing and Morning Star — enter on close. Hammer and Shooting Star — confirm wick held on the next candle before adding size.
